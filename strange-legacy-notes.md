# Strange Legacy Notes

* Buy The Rotting Roaster west of the capital early for a good source of
  income.
* The Desert Upgrade can be found in the Harbour City Workshop.
* The Water Upgrade is in Capital City.
* The Swamp Upgrade is at The Woodcutter.
* The Snow Upgrade is at Elves Rise.
* The Mountain Upgrade is in Swamp City.
* Carrying some luxury goods is dangerous.
* The Orphanage can be used to increase Fame.
* Grapevillage is to the northwest of Capital City.
* The sewers of Capital City are a good place to grind XP.
* Cheating level of at least 10 is needed to enter the Royal Hunter Society.
